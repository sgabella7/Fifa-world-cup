History of the FIFA World Cup

Timeline

  1. 1930–1950: The beginning
    • First tournament held in Uruguay (1930)
    • Early dominance by Uruguay, Italy, and others
    • 1942 & 1946 tournaments not held due to World War II
  2. 1954–1978: Global growth
    • Rise of new powers and iconic stars
    • Wider global viewership and tournament expansion
  3. 1982–1998: Modern era forms
    • More teams, more matches, bigger audiences
    • Growing influence of tactics, coaching, and sports science
  4. 2002–Present: Technology & global spotlight
    • More host nations across continents
    • VAR introduced (Video Assistant Referee)
    • Massive media and sponsorship growth
